About Me

Hi, I’m Sophie Reavis — a designer, researcher, and student-athlete passionate about digital media, interactive design, and storytelling. My work spans graphic design, branding, and multimedia installations, often blending technology and creativity to explore new ways of communicating ideas.

I’m currently studying at UCSB, where I balance Division I athletics with creative practice. Outside of school, I’ve worked on professional branding projects, research-driven visualizations, and interactive art. I hope to continue combining design with human-centered problem solving in future work.
