The Moon Illuminates What the Reef Remembers

Immersive installation • sound • scent • light

The Moon Illuminates What the Reef Remembers is a commentary on the ocean as a vast world we still understand only in fragments. While the surface shimmers with beauty, devastating changes like coral bleaching are unfolding below and demand our attention.

The installation layers multiple senses to build an eerie, immersive experience. A haunting soundtrack fills the room as projections ripple and shift in the background. Each coral is paired with a distinct scent—healthy coral carries a sweet fragrance, and each subsequent stage grows harsher until the dead coral smells unmistakably of decay.
