Vascular Biosciences

Graphic Design — Merch & Diagrams • 2023–Present

Overview

A series of designs for Vascular Biosciences spanning merchandise and technical diagrams. Work includes a t-shirt graphic, a bucket hat logo, and a set of Adobe Illustrator diagrams created for various projects. The goal was to keep the visual system clear, professional, and production-ready across apparel and scientific communication.

Merchandise

Technical Diagrams

Diagrams built in Adobe Illustrator to clarify mechanisms, workflows, and component relationships for ongoing projects. Designed for clarity at both slide scale and print scale.

Notes

Deliverables were prepared with print specs in mind (CMYK variants, safe margins, and outlined text where needed). For merch, files were exported as high-resolution raster or vector depending on vendor requirements. While many projects are not for public use, these images were approved to be displayed.
